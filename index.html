Our Story

Traditional recipes,
intergalactic standards.

Döner this good shouldn't exist on Earth. We stack halal beef and chicken by hand every morning, season it the way it's been done in Turkey for generations, and roast it slowly on the vertical spit until the edges crisp.

No shortcuts, no frozen cones, no earthly compromises. Just bread baked soft, sauces made in-house, and vegetables cut fresh daily.

Good Questions

From our transmissions.

Is Alien Döner halal?
Yes — all of our beef and chicken döner is 100% halal, carved fresh to order from the vertical spit.
Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?
Yes. Our hummus, babaganoush, lentil soup, spiced potatoes, and rice pilaf are vegetarian, and several are vegan-friendly. Just ask when you order.
Where is Alien Döner located?
Two Orange County spots: 560 W 19th St in Costa Mesa and 24301 Muirlands Blvd in Lake Forest.
Can I order online for pickup?
Absolutely. Order online for Costa Mesa or Lake Forest, or call (949) 873-6637.
What exactly is döner kebab?
Döner is Turkish-style meat (beef or chicken) stacked on a vertical rotisserie and slow-roasted, then thinly carved — the Turkish cousin of shawarma and gyro. We serve it in wraps, over rice, and on bread.
