The Costa Mesa Ship

Traditional Turkish döner, open late in Costa Mesa.

Our Costa Mesa kitchen on West 19th Street stacks halal beef and chicken by hand every morning and slow-roasts it on the vertical spit all day. Whether you're after an iskender drenched in brown-butter and tomato sauce, a loaded döner wrap, or meteor bites over fries, it's carved fresh when you order.

We're an easy stop for anyone in Costa Mesa, Newport Beach, Santa Ana, and Huntington Beach — with patio seating (dogs welcome) and a kitchen that stays open past midnight, later than almost anything else nearby. Late-night halal cravings, consider them handled.

New here? Start with the full menu, or beam an order straight to the kitchen for pickup.

Costa Mesa FAQ

Before you land.

What are your Costa Mesa hours?
Sunday–Thursday 11:00 AM–12:00 AM (midnight), and Friday–Saturday 11:00 AM–2:00 AM. We're one of the later-night halal spots in the area.
Is there parking near 560 W 19th St?
Yes — there's street parking along 19th Street plus nearby lot parking. It's a quick in-and-out for pickup orders.
Is the Costa Mesa location halal?
100%. All of our beef and chicken döner is halal, carved fresh to order.
Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes — hummus, babaganoush, lentil soup, spiced potatoes and rice pilaf. Several are vegan-friendly.
