The Lake Forest Ship

Traditional Turkish döner, fresh in Lake Forest.

Our Lake Forest kitchen on Muirlands Boulevard hand-stacks halal beef and chicken every morning and slow-roasts it on the vertical spit. Order it as a classic iskender with brown-butter and tomato sauce, a loaded döner wrap, or piled over buttery rice pilaf — always carved fresh to order.

We're a convenient halal stop for Lake Forest, Mission Viejo, Irvine, Laguna Hills, and Foothill Ranch, with patio seating where dogs are welcome. Open every day from 11 AM to 9 PM.

Lake Forest FAQ

Before you land.

What are your Lake Forest hours?
Open every day, 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM.
Is there parking at 24301 Muirlands Blvd?
Yes — there's plaza lot parking right at the location, easy for pickup.
Is the Lake Forest location halal?
100%. All of our beef and chicken döner is halal, carved fresh to order.
Do you have vegetarian options?
Yes — hummus, babaganoush, lentil soup, spiced potatoes and rice pilaf. Several are vegan-friendly.
